About Us

Cirrhosis Patient Survival (CPS) prediction is a field that uses medical data analysis to estimate the likelihood of a patient with cirrhosis surviving for a certain amount of time. It's important to note that these are predictions, not guarantees.

Our Mission: We are dedicated to empowering patients with cirrhosis and their caregivers by providing a tool to predict patient survival rates. Through advanced medical data analysis, we aim to bring clarity and information to the often-uncertain path of living with cirrhosis.

What We Do: Our cirrhosis survival prediction tool leverages complex medical data to estimate a patient's likelihood of survival over a specific timeframe with the use of the Naive Bayes model.

Important Disclaimer: It's important to remember that our tool is a prediction, not a guarantee. Our mission is to provide information, not replace the critical role of a physician in making treatment decisions. We highly recommend consulting with a doctor to discuss your specific situation and receive personalized care.
